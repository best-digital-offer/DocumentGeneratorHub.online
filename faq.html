Export & Download

What file formats can I export to?

Most generators support multiple export formats:

  • PDF: High-quality, print-ready PDF documents
  • Word (DOCX): Editable Microsoft Word documents
  • Excel (XLSX): Spreadsheet format for data-heavy documents
  • Plain Text: Simple text format
  • HTML: Web format for online use
  • Image (PNG/JPEG): Some certificates and awards

Available formats vary by generator type. Most business documents support PDF, Word, and Excel exports.

Is the PDF export print-ready?

Yes, all PDF exports are print-ready with:

  • Proper Margins: Standard margins for A4/US Letter paper
  • High Resolution: 300 DPI for crisp printing
  • Embedded Fonts: All fonts embedded for consistent display
  • Color Management: Optimized for both color and black & white printing
  • Page Setup: Proper page orientation and size

You can directly print the PDF files or send them to professional printers.

Can I edit the documents after downloading?

Yes, depending on the export format:

  • Word (DOCX): Fully editable in Microsoft Word or free alternatives
  • Excel (XLSX): Fully editable in Excel or spreadsheet software
  • PDF: Requires PDF editing software, but text remains selectable
  • Plain Text/HTML: Easily editable in any text editor

We recommend exporting to Word format if you need to make frequent edits.

Do downloads include watermarks?

No, we don't add watermarks to your documents. What you get:

  • Clean Documents: No watermarks or logos added
  • Professional Output: Documents look like you created them professionally
  • Brand Neutral: No Document Generator Hub branding on your documents
  • Commercial Use Ready: Suitable for business and professional use

Your documents are yours alone, with no branding from our platform.

What if the download doesn't work?

If you're having trouble downloading:

  1. Check Browser Settings: Ensure downloads are not blocked
  2. Try Different Browser: Some browsers handle downloads better
  3. Clear Cache: Clear browser cache and try again
  4. Disable Extensions: Temporarily disable ad-blockers or download managers
  5. Check Storage: Ensure you have enough storage space
  6. Try Different Format: If PDF doesn't work, try Word or Excel

If problems persist, please contact us through our contact page.
