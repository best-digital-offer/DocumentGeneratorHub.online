Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Privacy Summary

At Document Generator Hub, we prioritize your privacy. We don't store your documents, don't require registration, and process everything locally in your browser. This policy explains our minimal data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it:

  • Contact Forms: Name, email address, and message content when you contact us
  • Feedback: Comments and suggestions you submit
  • Document Data: Information you enter into our generators (processed locally only)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We collect minimal technical information:

  • Usage Analytics: Page views, time spent, and general usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, IP address (for security purposes)
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for website functionality

1.3 Information We Don't Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Personal documents or their content
  • Financial information or payment details
  • Social media profiles or login credentials
  • Location data beyond country-level for analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Document Generation

All document generation happens locally in your browser:

  • Your document data never leaves your device
  • We don't store or access your document content
  • Processing is done client-side for maximum privacy

2.2 Website Improvement

We use anonymized data to:

  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Understand which generators are most popular
  • Fix technical issues and bugs
  • Develop new features based on usage patterns

2.3 Communication

We use contact information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send important updates about our services
  • Notify you of significant changes to our policies

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Processing

Our privacy-first approach includes:

  • Client-Side Processing: All document generation happens in your browser
  • No Server Storage: We don't store your documents on our servers
  • Temporary Storage: Some data may be temporarily stored in browser memory
  • Automatic Clearing: Data clears when you close your browser

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All connections are encrypted
  • Secure Hosting: Our website is hosted on secure, monitored servers
  • Regular Updates: We keep our systems updated with security patches
  • Access Controls: Limited access to any collected data

4. Cookies and Tracking

4.1 Essential Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for:

  • Website functionality and navigation
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Security and fraud prevention

4.2 Analytics Cookies

We use privacy-respecting analytics:

  • Google Analytics with anonymized IP addresses
  • No cross-site tracking or profiling
  • Aggregated, non-personal usage statistics only

4.3 Advertising Cookies

For AdSense compliance:

  • Google AdSense may place cookies for ad personalization
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your Google settings
  • We don't control third-party advertising cookies

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google Services

We use Google services with privacy considerations:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymized website analytics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements
  • Google Fonts: For website typography (cached locally when possible)

5.2 Hosting and CDN

Our website infrastructure:

  • Secure web hosting with privacy-compliant providers
  • Content Delivery Network (CDN) for faster loading
  • All providers comply with international privacy standards

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Data Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

6.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through:

  • Browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Opt-out tools provided by advertising networks
  • Privacy settings in your Google account

6.3 Communication Preferences

You can:

  • Unsubscribe from any communications we send
  • Update your contact preferences
  • Request to stop receiving certain types of messages

7. Children's Privacy

Our service and privacy practices regarding children:

  • We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13
  • Our services are designed for general audiences
  • Parents can contact us to request deletion of a child's information
  • We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

8. International Data Transfers

Regarding data transfers:

  • We primarily process data within your browser (no transfer needed)
  • Any server-side data is processed in secure, compliant data centers
  • We ensure adequate protection for any international transfers
  • We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws

9. Data Retention

Our data retention practices:

  • Document Data: Not stored (processed locally only)
  • Contact Information: Retained until you request deletion
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for up to 26 months
  • Technical Logs: Retained for security purposes for up to 12 months

10. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Consent: When you voluntarily provide information
  • Legitimate Interest: For website analytics and improvement
  • Legal Obligation: When required by law
  • Contract Performance: To provide our services

11. Changes to This Policy

Regarding policy updates:

  • We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices
  • Material changes will be prominently posted on our website
  • We'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Continued use of our service constitutes acceptance of changes

12. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@documentgeneratorhub.online
  • Contact Form: Use our contact page
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

13. Compliance and Certifications

We comply with:

  • GDPR: European General Data Protection Regulation
  • CCPA: California Consumer Privacy Act
  • COPPA: Children's Online Privacy Protection Act
  • SOC 2: Our hosting providers maintain SOC 2 compliance

Our Privacy Commitment

At Document Generator Hub, privacy isn't an afterthought—it's built into our core design. By processing documents locally in your browser and minimizing data collection, we ensure your sensitive information stays private and secure.

We believe in transparency, user control, and responsible data practices. If you have any questions about our privacy practices or need assistance with your privacy rights, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

We're committed to transparency and protecting your privacy. Contact us if you need clarification on any aspect of our privacy practices.
